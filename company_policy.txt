Sapphirus Systems Employee Handbook

1. Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 20 working days per year, accruable up to 30 days.
- Sick leave: 14 days per year with a medical certificate required for absences over 3 consecutive days.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks fully paid maternity leave, 4 weeks paternity leave.
- Public holidays: As per the national calendar, plus 2 floating holidays.

2. Work Hours
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Remote work: Up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Flexitime: Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM; remaining hours flexible.

3. Code of Conduct
- All employees must sign the confidentiality agreement upon joining.
- Personal use of company resources must not exceed 5% of work time.
- Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.

4. IT & Security
- Passwords must be rotated every 90 days.
- Company laptops must use full-disk encryption.
- VPN is mandatory when accessing company resources from outside the office.

5. Travel Policy
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours, business class for flights over 6 hours.
- Daily meal allowance: $50 for domestic travel, $75 for international.
- Hotel: 4-star or equivalent, not to exceed $250/night domestic, $350/night international.

6. Performance Reviews
- Quarterly reviews with direct manager.
- Annual bonus calculated as 10-20% of base salary based on performance rating.
- Promotion eligibility reviewed annually in December.
